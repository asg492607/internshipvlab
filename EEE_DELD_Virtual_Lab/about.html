About EEE Virtual Lab

Purpose

This virtual laboratory provides a structured digital platform for Electrical and Electronics Engineering experiments. It helps students study theory, understand components, perform simulations, save observations and complete assessments.

Objectives

To improve practical understanding by using professional circuit schematics, formula-based calculations and experiment-wise observation tables.

Features

TheoryClear concepts and formulas
ComponentsApparatus table with images
SimulationUnique circuit schematic per experiment
AssessmentQuiz and progress tracking
